Welcome to Olympion Hotel

Experience luxury, comfort, and authentic Greek hospitality at our hotel on the beautiful island of Thassos, where modern amenities and warm service ensure a relaxing stay.

About Us

Located in the picturesque village of Potos, our hotel is just a short walk from the crystal-clear waters of the Aegean Sea and surrounded by lush green hills.
Thassos, known as the "Emerald Island" of the North Aegean, is famous for its pristine beaches, traditional villages, and rich history, making it the perfect destination.
Hotel Olympion was founded with a vision to offer guests a peaceful retreat where they can reconnect with nature and experience authentic Greek hospitality.
Our hotel combines modern comforts with a touch of traditional charm, providing a warm atmosphere for all who visit.

Where to find us?

Hotel Olympion is in the heart of Potos, 45km south of Limenas, the main port of Thassos. You can reach us by ferry from the mainland and then by car, taxi, or bus. The main road passes nearby, providing easy access. Check the map for exact directions.
